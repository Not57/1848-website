By The Numbers

Trades Pay. Trades Grow. Trades Last.

The data is clear — skilled trades are among the fastest-growing, highest-demand careers in America.

$51K median welder salary
2.1M skilled trades jobs unfilled by 2030
$0 student debt to train at 1848
11% projected electrician job growth

Sources: BLS — Welders Occupational Outlook · BLS — Electricians Outlook · Deloitte & The Manufacturing Institute

Real Skills. Reliable Wages.

Welders typically start at $20 to $25/hr. The trade is in high demand to replace a retiring workforce, and on-site physical labor is nearly impossible to outsource.

Helping students turn adversity into opportunity.

Students & Youth

Futureproof your career without the debt. Learn skills that matter and build something real — from welding certifications to entrepreneurship, starting as young as nine years old. A welding cert opens doors across construction, aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing — industries that are always hiring.

Neurodivergent Learners

40% of 1848 students have ADHD. 10% are on the autism spectrum. That's not a coincidence — ADHD and autism come with exceptional spatial awareness, hyper-focus, and mechanical aptitude. The exact traits trades reward. 1848 teaches the way people actually learn.

Veterans & Career Changers

Your service built discipline. 1848 builds the next chapter. Pivot into a high-demand trade — new certification, new income, new purpose — in weeks, not years.

Entrepreneurs & Retirees

Turn craft into a business, or simply stay sharp and connected. 1848 is a lifelong community — there is no age limit on purpose.
